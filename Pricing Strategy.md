### **Key Considerations**
1. **Market Research:** Research competitors in your area to align pricing with industry standards.  
2. **Experience Premium:** Her extensive experience (e.g., founding a school) justifies pricing at the higher end of the range.  
3. **Bundling:** Offer discounts for bundled services (e.g., a curriculum design + teacher training package).  
4. **Flexibility:** Adjust rates for nonprofits, small schools, or underserved communities if desired.
